The Pokédex states:

Gen 1:

Shoots water at prey while in the water. Withdraws into it's shell when in danger.

Gen 2:

The shell, which hardens soon after it is born, is resiliant. If you poke it, it will bounce back out.

Gen 3:

It's shell is not just for protection. It's rounded shape and the grooves on it's surface minimize resistance in water, enabling squirtle to swim at high speeds.

Gen 4:

It shelters itself in it's shell, then strikes back with spouts of water at every oppurtunity.
