The Pokédex states:

Gen 1:

The flame at the tip of it's tail makes a sound as it burns. You can only hear it in quiet places.

Gen 2:

If it is healthy, the flame on the tip of it's tail will burn vigorously, even if it gets a bit wet.

Gen 3:

The flame that burns at the tip of it's tail is an indication of it's emotion's. The flame wavers when Charmander is enjoying itself. If the pokemon becomes enraged, the flame burns fiercely.

Gen 4:

The fire on the tip of it's tail is a measure of it's life. If healthy, it's tail burn intensely.
