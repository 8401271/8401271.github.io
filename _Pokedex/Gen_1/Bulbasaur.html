The Pokédex states:

Gen 1:

A strange seed was planted on its back at birth. The plant sprouts and grows with this Pokémon.

Gen 2:

The seed on its back is filled with nutrients. The seed grows steadily larger as its body grows.

Gen 3:

Bulbasaur can be seen napping in bright sulight. There is a seed on its back. by soaking up the the sun's rays, the seed grows progressively larger.

Gen 4:

For some time after it's birth, it grows by gaining nourishment from the seed on it's back.
